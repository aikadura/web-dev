Finn the Human

    Finn debuted at age 12 but has aged over the course of the show. He is currently 17 as of season 10. Finn sets off on magical random quests around the Land of Ooo.

    Jake the Dog

    Finn's best friend and adoptive brother. He is a shape-shifting dog who is in his 30s in "magical dog years". Jake can stretch, shrink, or mold any part of his body.

    Princess Bubblegum

    The current incarnation of the Candy Elemental, comparable to the inhabitants of the Candy Kingdom, who are all composed of types of desserts and candies.

    Lumpy Space Princess

    LSP is spoiled and sarcastic, and is often shown living outdoors with salvaged furniture and appears to be homeless, because she has run away from her parents.

    Marceline the Vampire Queen

    Marceline becomes a close friend to Finn and Jake. Several backstory episodes have established that she was born to an unnamed human mother and the demon Hunson Abadeer.

    Flame Princess

    Like all other inhabitants of the Fire Kingdom, she is covered with fire, the very flames emitted from her body being extensions of herself that intensify when she becomes emotionally excited.
